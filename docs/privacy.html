Privacy Policy

WiFi QR Scanner — Last updated March 2026

WiFi QR Scanner is a free, open-source Windows utility. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

Camera

The app accesses your webcam to scan QR codes. Camera frames are processed locally on your device and are never stored, recorded, or transmitted anywhere.

WiFi credentials

When a WiFi QR code is scanned, the network name (SSID) and password are passed directly to the Windows WiFi API to connect your device. Credentials are not stored by the app, logged, or sent to any server. Windows may save the network profile as part of its normal WiFi management.

Current network (Share WiFi feature)

The app reads your currently connected network name from Windows to display a shareable QR code. This information never leaves your device.

No analytics or tracking

The app collects no usage data, telemetry, crash reports, or analytics of any kind. It makes no network requests.

Contact

Questions or concerns? Open an issue on GitHub.
